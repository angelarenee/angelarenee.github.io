Loya Woodworks

Loya Woodworks specializes in bold and solid structures. Crafted to create functional furniture, unique home accents, and custom pieces.

It all started in 1990 with a bookcase made from discarded crates found in the back of a grocery store. Alex Loya's has expanded his craft. Today, Loya Woodworks offers:

  • chairs
  • patio decks
  • tables
  • lamps
  • unique art pieces
